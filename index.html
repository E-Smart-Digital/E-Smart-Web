Money Transfer

Send money to anyone,
anywhere in SA

Instantly send money to any South African phone number. No bank account required for you or the recipient.

Fast, Secure & Simple

Whether you're sending money to family in another province or paying for a local service, E-Smart makes it effortless.

  • Instant Delivery: Funds are available to the recipient immediately.
  • No Bank Account Needed: Use just a phone number to send and receive.
  • Low Flat Fees: Know exactly what you pay with our transparent pricing.
  • Secure PIN: Every transaction is protected by your personal secret PIN.

How to send money

1

Dial *120*3274# or open the E-Smart app.

2

Select 'Send Money' and enter the recipient's phone number.

3

Enter the amount and confirm with your secure PIN.

eVouchers

Cash out at any ATM,
no card needed

Generate a secure eVoucher code on your phone and withdraw cash at any major bank ATM across South Africa.

Cash in Hand, Instantly

Need physical cash? E-Smart eVouchers allow you to withdraw your balance from any ATM without needing a bank card.

  • Universal Access: Works at Absa, FNB, Standard Bank, and Nedbank ATMs.
  • Cardless Convenience: No plastic card required, just your unique voucher code.
  • Flexible Amounts: Withdraw exactly what you need, from R50 to R5000.
  • Secure PIN: Every transaction is protected by your personal secret PIN.

How to use eVouchers

1

Dial *120*3274# or open the E-Smart app.

2

Select 'eVoucher' and enter the amount you wish to withdraw.

3

Receive your 10-digit voucher code via SMS instantly.
